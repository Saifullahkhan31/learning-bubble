Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by clicking the "Register Now" button and following the enrollment process. Our team will guide you through course selection and payment options.

What age groups do you serve?

We offer courses for K-12 students, as well as programs for adults including homemakers and professionals looking to enhance their skills.

Are classes live or recorded?

We offer both live interactive classes and recorded sessions. Live classes allow real-time interaction, while recorded sessions provide flexibility in scheduling.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our courses. These certificates can be used to demonstrate your learning achievements.

What technical requirements do I need?

You'll need a stable internet connection, a computer or tablet, and basic software like a web browser. We'll provide detailed technical requirements during enrollment.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee with our refund policy. Please contact our support team within the specified timeframe for refund requests.
